Build Windows Store Apps with BootstrapFTW

Like Bootstrap? Here's why you'll love it for Win apps:

Not From Micro$oft.

Build by a community of app developers, BootstrapFTW is an opinionated take on building Windows Store apps which isn't written by Microsoft employees. This gives us the freedom to take what we like and discard what we doesn't make sense.

Snap Proof.

Need a simple solution for Snapped-mode, half-mode, snapped-big-mode, portrait-mode (and the 17 new modes that will come out next release)? Hello, it's called "Responsive Design". It works great and it's built in.

Packed with features.

A Grid system built to match the Win8 'siloette' is built in as well as a curated collection of community components, typography, forms, and even a customizer.

Jumpstart with Templates.

For an even faster start, use one of these pre-build sample apps. Click on the picture to view a gallery for each.
